Fixed or controllable pitch

A Controllable pitch propellor (CPP) has blades that can be adjusted to vary the thrust power or direction. When a CPP is used, the shaft has to be pulled to install the seal or a new liner.


When using a fixed pitch propellor, the aft seal can be installed without pulling the shaft as removal of the propellor is enough.


VGP (Vessel general permit)

The VGP states that no mineral oil can be leaked or a specific bio-oil must be used. To comply with the VGP regulations, either bio-oil must be used or an air type (Ventus/Athmos) seal must be used.

Seaguard information

Ensures extra security against oil leakage into the water environment. The chamber between the two oil-repellant lip seals is filled with oil from the L.O. tank. The pressure on both sides of the fourth lip seal remains equal. By closing-off the oil supply to this chamber, this fourth lip seal can be used as a new oil-repellant lip seal. Benefits: In case of leakage, docking and maintenance can be postponed by putting this fourth seal to work

Information

HML

Tests under specific conditions indicate that the lifetime of a liner provided with a HML may be stretched up to 20 years. The real lifetime varies depending on the specific operation conditions of a sealk


Distance ring

The distance ring extends the lifetime of a liner. A divisible distance ring can be fitted with the SUPREME design. Over time, the rubber lip seals wear down the liner. By removing or adding a distance ring, the housing parts can be repositioned. After repositioning, the lip seals run on a new surface thus extending the lifetime of the seal.
