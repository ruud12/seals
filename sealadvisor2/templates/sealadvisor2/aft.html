Seaguard

- Do you prefer to be on the safe side?

Seaguard information

Ensures extra security against oil leakage into the water environment. The chamber between the two oil-repellant lip seals is filled with oil from the L.O. tank. The pressure on both sides of the fourth lip seal remains equal. By closing-off the oil supply to this chamber, this fourth lip seal can be used as a new oil-repellant lip seal. Benefits: In case of leakage, docking and maintenance can be postponed by putting this fourth seal to work

Zero emission using air system

Ventus
The Supreme Ventus uses an air system to separate oil and water and regulate pressures inside the shaft seal and stern tube. Any fluids that enter the shaft seal are drained into an internal drain tank. Different than most systems, the Supreme Ventus works without interventions of sensors and varying air-flows and it is fully self-regulatory.
The standard Supreme Ventus execution consists of bronze housing parts including the Supreme Ventus valve, a chrome steel liner, one oil and two water-repellant NBR lip seals. In addition the system uses a compact drain tank with self-regulatory air-control unit.

Athmos
The Supreme Athmos features a fail safe and Condition Monitoring option to deliver you optimal peace of mind. If the system fails, the seal will switch to its normal sealing mode. The drain tank captures the little oil that the seal uses and the tank indicates when it should be emptied. That way, the sealsâ€™ condition can be monitored at all times.

HML / Distance ring

- Would you like to stretch the liner lifetime using a HML (hard metal layer) or distance ring?

Information

HML

Tests under specific conditions indicate that the lifetime of a liner provided with a HML may be stretched up to 20 years. The real lifetime varies depending on the specific operation conditions of a seal.


Distance ring

The distance ring extends the lifetime of a liner. A divisible distance ring can be fitted with the SUPREME design. Over time, the rubber lip seals wear down the liner. By removing or adding a distance ring, the housing parts can be repositioned. After repositioning, the lip seals run on a new surface thus extending the lifetime of the seal.
